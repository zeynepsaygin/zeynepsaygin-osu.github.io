Longitudinal MRI in Youth Hockey Players: The LACES Youth Hockey Study

  • Participate in non-invasive MRI research to take pictures of your child’s brain to record brain activity while they are performing simple cognitive and perceptual tasks and to understand connections between brain areas.
  • The objective is to find out how the brain changes during a season of youth hockey, so we will scan your child both before and after the season.
  • You will be reimbursed for your time with increasing compensation for each subsequent visit. And you will get pictures of your child’s brain like the images above!
  • To sign up, contact us at zlab.subs@osu.edu


Infant Longitudinal MRI

  • Participate in non-invasive MRI research to take pictures of your infant's brain while they are asleep! And when they are older, we will take pictures of your child’s brain while they are watching colorful images and while they are hearing different sounds in the scanner.
  • We are looking for children between 0 - 1 years of age. You will be reimbursed for your time with increasing compensation for each subsequent visit. You will also get pictures of your child’s brain like the images above!
  • To sign up, contact us at zlab.subs@osu.edu


Child Longitudinal MRI

  • Participate in non-invasive MRI research to take pictures of your child’s brain during simple cognitive and perceptual tasks, to understand connections between brain areas, how they change across time, and how they make your child unique!
  • We are looking for children between 3 – 6 years of age. You will be reimbursed for your time with increasing compensation for each subsequent visit. You will also get pictures of your child’s brain like the images above!
  • To sign up, contact us at zlab.subs@osu.edu


Young Adult MRI Study

  • Participate in non-invasive MRI research to take pictures of your brain while performing fun cognitive and perceptual tasks, to understand how brain connectivity relates to neural activity!
  • We are looking for adults between 18 – 40 years of age. You will be reimbursed for your time with increasing compensation for each subsequent visit. You will also get a picture of your brain after the first visit!
  • To sign up, contact us at zlab.subs@osu.edu
